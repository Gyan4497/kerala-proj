ABOUT BPA SYSTEM

Digitization of entire process

Open Source,Smart System

Standardization of approval process

Automated workflows,reduced turn around time

  • It involves manual to complete automation and transparent process.Reads CAD drawing and produces scrutiny reports in a few minutes, mapping all the development control rules of the Authority to the drawing entities. It automates the lengthy and cumbersome manual process of checking the development regulations, thus reducing paper work, valuable time and effort of Architects and the Authority.Reducing the turnaround time for approvals that a ULB offers for procurement of licenses and certificates by business users.

    Stake holders like ULB officials, architects Town planners, engineers and citizen are connected on a single platform for various services like new construction, addition or alteration, demolition, etc.Stakeholders are updated on real time of the application progress and brings in complete transparency.

    State-of- the-art, ERP-based platform – for urban governance is built on advanced open source technologies for better flexibility, interoperability and faster implementation .

    Existing manual process is completely replaced with automated workflow.Entire process is transparent with well-defined roles and responsibilities of various users which are mapped in the system, with complete audit trails. MIS reports are generated to help track process efficiency.

    Unlike traditional “Building Plan Licensing systems”, the DCR is inbuilt.Auto synchronization of data between DIGIT DCR and BPA application.
